WikaBlanks Instructions

How to Play WikaBlanks

  1. Read the Sentence: A sentence with a blank will appear on the screen.
  2. Choose the Correct Word: Select the correct word from the four choices provided to fill in the blank.
  3. Submit Your Answer: Click on the word you think is correct.
  4. See the Result: You'll see if your answer is correct or wrong, along with the definition of the correct word.
  5. Next Sentence: Click "Next" to move to the next sentence.
  6. Complete the Game: Finish all 10 sentences to see your final score!
